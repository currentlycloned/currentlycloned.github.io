About Me

I’m a bilingual creator based in Ottawa with a strong interest in multimedia design, creative coding, and game development. I’ve been coding since early high school and quickly fell in love with using code as a creative tool — especially for games, interactive stories, and visual art.

My work spans digital illustration, animation, and programming, and I’m especially drawn to projects that combine narrative, atmosphere, and thoughtful design.
